Grand Opening

After a year of creation, videos, coding, scripting, and much more. The Unknown SMP is finally opening its threshold to the first ever test subjects on November 1st 2023! The Grand Opening is the first in-game event streamed live on Twitch, exclusively on twitch.tv/lumaa_dev by one of the owners of the Unknown SMP, Lumaa. The event's length is around 5 to 10 minutes and will be reposted on YouTube as both a Twitch VOD and as a roleplay-ish video.

If you are a viewer/subscriber of the Unknown SMP, consider putting an alarm at 9:30 PM (UTC+2) and watch the livestream!

If you are a player/test subject, you have a few rules to follow to maintain a good environment on both the livestream and on the server.

Rules and notices for players

If you plan on playing on the Unknown SMP, here are the rules and notices you need to know:

1. The mods required are "The Backrooms Mod", "USMP Tapes" and "Simple Voice Chat" which are all available on Modrinth.
2. There is a recommended modpack, "Unknown SMP" on Modrinth.

3.1. Rule #1: Cheats, hacks and exploits are forbidden and will result in a 3-day ban.
3.2. Rule #2: The IP must remain private among the players.
3.3. Rule #3: Feel free to make content on the server!
3.4. Rule #4: Respect the other members as well as their boundaries.

If you are going to participate to the event, here are the rules to follow only during the event:

1. You have 5 hours before the beginning to configurate all the required mods, after 5 hours the event will begin wether or not all players are ready.
2. No hacks or exploit are allowed to get to restricted areas.
3. No breaking or attempts of breaking builds or decorations.
4. Follow the 2 scientists (Lumaa and Patate) the most you can. If you try and go somewhere unallowed, a force field will be activated.
